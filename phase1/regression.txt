MSE ko kis se compare karte hain?
1️⃣ Target value ke scale se

Data kis range ka hai?

Same MSE chhote scale me high, bade scale me low ho sakta hai

2️⃣ Baseline model se

Mean prediction se better hai ya nahi

Agar baseline se better nahi → model bekaar

3️⃣ Train vs Test error se

Gap zyada → overfitting

Dono high → underfitting

4️⃣ Domain expectation se

Business / real-life tolerance kya hai

Example:

Exam marks ±5 ok

Medical prediction me ±5 not ok
